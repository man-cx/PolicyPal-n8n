General Questions
What is PolicyPal?
PolicyPal is a comprehensive insurance policy management app designed to help you store, organize, and manage all your insurance policies in one place. It provides features such as document storage, policy sharing with family members, and an AI advisor to answer questions about your coverage.
How do I create an account?
To create an account, download the PolicyPal app from the App Store or Google Play Store, then click on "Register" on the welcome screen. You'll need to provide your email address and create a password. After verifying your email, you can set up your profile and start adding your insurance policies.
Is my data secure?
Yes, SecurityPal takes data security very seriously. All your data is encrypted both during transmission and while stored on our servers. We use industry-standard security protocols and regular security audits to ensure your information remains private and protected. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.
What types of insurance policies can I manage?
PolicyPal supports a wide range of insurance types including health, life, auto, home, travel, business, and more. If you have a policy type that doesn't fit into our predefined categories, you can use the "Other" category and customize it to your needs.
How do I add a new policy?
To add a new policy, navigate to the Policies tab and tap the "+" button. Select the policy type, then fill in the details like insurer name, policy number, coverage amounts, and dates. You can also upload related documents such as policy contracts or insurance cards. Once completed, tap "Save" to add the policy to your account.
How can I share my policy with family members?
To share a policy, go to the Sharing tab or open the specific policy and tap the "Share" button. Enter the email address of the person you want to share with and select their permission level (view-only, edit, or full access). They will receive an invitation to create an account if they don't already have one, and the policy will appear in their account once they accept.
What is the AI Policy Advisor?
The AI Policy Advisor is an intelligent assistant that can answer questions about your insurance policies. It analyzes your policy details to provide personalized information about coverage, deductibles, exclusions, and more. You can ask questions in natural language, such as "Does my health insurance cover dental?" or "When is my auto policy renewal date?"
Is PolicyPal free to use?
PolicyPal offers a free basic plan that allows you to store up to 3 policies with limited features. For full access to all features, including unlimited policies, advanced AI advisor capabilities, and priority support, you can upgrade to PolicyPal Premium for a monthly or annual subscription fee. See our pricing page for current rates and promotional offers.
